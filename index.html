Recipe of the Day

Sesame Chicken

What You'll Need

  • 6 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
  • 1/2 cup of honey
  • 1/2 cup of soy sauce
  • 1 cup of water
  • 2 tablespoons of cornstarch
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon of red pepper flakes
  • 1 tablespoon of toasted sesame seeds

Directions

  1. First, cut chicken breast into 1 inch strips or bite size pieces.
  2. Next, heat a large non-stick skillet that has been sprayed with non-stick spray , over medium-high heat.
  3. Cook chicken for about 6 minutes or until no longer pink.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix together honey, soy sauce, water, corn starch, ginger and red pepper flakes. Then, whisk until no corn starch lumps appear.
  5. Heat 1 tbsp oil in wok; add selected meat to wok, along with carrots, peas, and cooked onion; stir-fry for 2 minutes. Add rice, green onions, and bean sprouts, tossing to mix well; stir-fry for 3 minutes.
  6. Pour sauce mixture into skillet with chicken, and cook until the sauce thickens slightly. Then, sprinkle with sesame seeds.
  7. Cover and simmer for 10 minutes or until chicken starts to soak up the sauce.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
