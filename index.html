Recipe of the Day

Sesame Chicken

What You'll Need

  • 6 cut chicken strips
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 3 drops soy sauce
  • 3 drops sesame oil
  • 8 ounces cooked chicken, chopped
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed
  • 4 cups cold cooked rice
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 2 cups bean sprouts
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

Directions

  1. First, heat 1 tbsp oil in wok; add chopped onions and stir-fry until onions turn a nice brown color (should take about 8-10 minutes). Then, remove from wok.
  2. Allow wok to cool slightly.
  3. Next, mix the egg with 3 drops of soy and 3 drops of sesame oil, then set aside.
  4. Add 1/2 tbsp oil to wok, swirling to coat surfaces; add egg mixture; working quickly, swirl egg until egg sets against wok; when egg puffs, flip egg and cook other side briefly; remove from wok, and chop into small pieces.
  5. Heat 1 tbsp oil in wok; add selected meat to wok, along with carrots, peas, and cooked onion; stir-fry for 2 minutes. Add rice, green onions, and bean sprouts, tossing to mix well; stir-fry for 3 minutes.
  6. Add 2 tbsp of light soy sauce and chopped egg to rice mixture and fold in; stir-fry for 1 minute more
  7. Serve, and enjoy!
