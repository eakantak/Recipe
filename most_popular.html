Fried Rice

What You'll Need

3/4 cup finely chopped onion 2 1/2 tablespoons oil 1 egg, lightly beaten 3 drops soy sauce 3 drops sesame oil 8 ounces cooked chicken, chopped 1/2 cup finely chopped carrots 1/2 cup frozen peas, thawed 4 cups cold cooked rice 4 green onions, chopped 2 cups bean sprouts 2 tablespoons soy sauce

Directions

First, heat 1 tbsp oil in wok; add chopped onions and stir-fry until onions turn a nice brown color (should take about 8-10 minutes). Then, remove from wok. Allow wok to cool slightly. Next, mix the egg with 3 drops of soy and 3 drops of sesame oil, then set aside. Add 1/2 tbsp oil to wok, swirling to coat surfaces; add egg mixture; working quickly, swirl egg until egg sets against wok; when egg puffs, flip egg and cook other side briefly; remove from wok, and chop into small pieces. Heat 1 tbsp oil in wok; add selected meat to wok, along with carrots, peas, and cooked onion; stir-fry for 2 minutes. Add rice, green onions, and bean sprouts, tossing to mix well; stir-fry for 3 minutes. Add 2 tbsp of light soy sauce and chopped egg to rice mixture and fold in; stir-fry for 1 minute more Serve, and enjoy!
