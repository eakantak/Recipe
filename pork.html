Szechuan Pork

What You'll Need

  • 6 ounces soba noodles, uncooked
  • 2 teaspoons dark sesame oil
  • 1 lb pork tenderloin, trimmed and cut into 2-inch strips
  • 1 tablespoon chili garlic sauce
  • 1 teaspoon bottled ground fresh ginger
  • 3/4 cup red bell pepper strips (about 1 small pepper)
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth $
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 4 cups cold cooked rice
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  • 3/4 cup diagonally cut green onions (about 4 green onions)

Directions

  1. First, Cook noodles according to package directions. Drain and rinse with cold water, then drain.
  2. Next, heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add pork, chili garlic sauce, and ginger to pan; stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  4. Add bell pepper to pan; stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  5. Add broth, soy sauce, and peanut butter to pan.
  6. Add 2 tbsp of light soy sauce and chopped egg to rice mixture and fold in; stir-fry for 1 minute more
  7. Reduce heat to low; cook for 1 minute or until sauce is slightly thick.
  8. Stir in onions.
  9. Serve over noodles, and enjoy!
